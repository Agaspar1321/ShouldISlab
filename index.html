Know before you slab it.

Drop in a card's raw price and its PSA comps — we'll tell you if it's worth grading. Most calculators show you the best case: what you'd make if it gems a 10. Ours weighs the real odds of every grade, factors in fees and grading cost, and gives you your true expected profit — the number that actually matters. It finds your break-even grade and tells you straight when the smart move is to not grade at all.
